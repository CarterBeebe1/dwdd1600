Commonly asked questions:

What should I consider when choosing a gaming monitor?
Look for a monitor with a high refresh rate of at least 120hz for smooth gameplay. Find a higher resolution monitor for better clarity such as 1440p or 4k. Look for G-Sync/FreeSync compatable monitors for reduced stuttering and screen tearing.
How do I callibrate my monitor for color accuracy.
Use calibration tools such as Datacolor Spyder or X-Rite i1Display can help achieve color accuracy.
What should I do if my monitor has dead pixels
Software tools are available to fix stuck pixels by cycling colors rapidly on screen. Also try applying gentle pressure with a soft cloth to see if the pixel gets unstuck. Also check if the monitor is under warranty with a policy for replacing a monitor with a certain number of dead pixels.
